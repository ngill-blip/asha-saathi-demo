Clickable mock-up of the WhatsApp refresher tool

One training module (kitab) — TPT counselling: the 5-point talk — as an ASHA would receive it before a home visit. Tap the reply buttons to move through it. The voice notes and video really play.

Built on ASHA tech-engagement research

  • Clear start & end — every module sets context up front and closes with what comes next.
  • List menu + reply buttons — pick a kitab, then act on each step ("Aage badhen") instead of reading passively.
  • End-of-lesson summary — key points recapped at the close (the most-valued element in the research).
  • Scorecard + remediation — a visible score; a wrong answer re-serves the content, then a retry.
  • Plain, short text — minimal emoji; messages kept short to match reading pace.
  • Scheduled reminder — closes by offering the next kitab on a set day/evening (an anticipation loop).

What's real vs simulated: the audio is generated Hindi TTS and the video is an auto-built slide reel — stand-ins so the team can hear/see the format. A production build would use a recorded regional voice. The chat engine is a front-end mock, not a live WhatsApp connection. Structure adapted from learnings from Treemouse's HCM formative research, content guidelines and their previous learnings on WhatsApp based training tools.
